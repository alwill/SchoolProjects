The Martian (2009-present) Rating 8.3

During a manned mission to Mars, Astronaut Mark Watney is presumeddead after a fierce storm and left behind by his crew. But Watney has survived and finds himself stranded and alone on the hostileplanet. With only meager supplies, he must draw upon his ingenuity, wit and spirit to subsist and find a way to signal to Earth that he is alive.
